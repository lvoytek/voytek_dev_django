Lena's Cool Ciabatta

Ingredients
  • 1000g Flour
  • 770g Water
  • 1 tsp Instant Yeast
  • 24g Salt
Makes 4 loaves or 16 rolls.
Directions

Start the process by creating a poolish:

  • Mix 100g of flour with 1 tsp of instant yeast
  • Add 100g of water, stir to combine
  • Cover bowl and wait 4 hours

Add the remaining ingredients:

  • Add 720g of water, stir to combine
  • Add 24g of salt, stir again
  • Add 900g of flour in 100-200g increments, mixing each time
  • Cover the dough ball in the bowl and wait for 30 min

Now it's time to stretch and fold:

  • With wet hands, stretch the dough by pulling it toward you, then flop the closer half onto the other.
  • Rotate the bowl by a quarter turn
  • Repeat above two steps 11-19 more times to smooth the dough
  • Cover the bowl and wait for 30 min
  • Repeat the above steps three more times, waiting 2 hours after the final round

Prepare to wait:

  • With wet hands, remove the dough from the bowl
  • Punch the dough down, then ball it back up
  • Return the dough to the bowl, cover, and move to the fridge
  • Wait overnight for the dough to rise again

Prepare to bake:

  • Remove dough from bowl and ball it up
  • Divide dough into 4 equal portions then ball them all up
  • Sprinkle work surface with a lot of flour
  • Roll each ball to cover it in flour
  • If you would like to make rolls, subdivide balls into 4 equal parts each and sprinkle with flour
  • Cover with towel and wait 2 and a half hours

  • Place balls onto pans lined with parchment paper

  • Cool for 20 min
